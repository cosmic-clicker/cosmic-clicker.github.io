How to Play

  1. Click the "Click for QP" button to gather Quantum Particles (QP).
  2. Buy Upgrades to increase your QP per click.
  3. Buy Automated Collectors to generate QP over time, even when you're not clicking!
  4. Cosmic Collapse:
    • Once you have enough QP (at least 1000), click "Cosmic Collapse" to reset your QP, upgrades, and collectors.
    • You will gain a Singularity Shard (SS) for each collapse.
    • Each SS gives you a permanent 5% multiplicative bonus to your base QP per click.
    • You also get a scaling bonus based on how much QP you had when you collapsed. The more QP, the bigger the bonus! This encourages you to reach higher amounts before collapsing.
    • Your Energy Per Click will be significantly boosted after each collapse, allowing you to progress much faster.
  5. Power the Singularity Engine: Contribute QP to the engine to reach a major milestone.
  6. The Goal: Power the Singularity Engine to its maximum capacity. This will take a LOT of time and dedication!
  7. Tip: Experiment with different upgrade and collector combinations to find the most efficient way to gather QP.
